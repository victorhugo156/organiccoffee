How does it works?

Knowing the main stages of the coffee production process is the first step for us to understand the real importance of each one's work - from the producer to the roaster, from the barista to the consumer - in this chain where no one is supporting. All have their main role in obtaining a high added value coffee.

We learned a lot and we would like to share all our knowledge to benefit and add more quality to coffee lovers. We personally mine the best microlots of specialty coffees in the best coffee regions in South America. We believe and trust in the processes, we value the entire coffee cycle and respect all the protagonists, until they reach the final consumer. Thus, in addition to adding more quality to the coffee, we also add origin and history.

Choosing the best seedling, correct soil preparation, planting, fertilizing, fighting diseases, developing the feet for 3/4 years until harvest (influenced by heat, altitude and the variety of each type), drying, processing and re-beneficiation (hulling, cleaning and classification of coffee). Where it all starts.

Understanding the roasting process is an art
that requires technical knowledge and expertise

The technique. The beans, the types of roaster, the roasting profiles developed for each batch (influenced by temperature and time), the cupping (the tasting) and sensory analysis (color, body, aroma, acidity, taste, aftertaste), the adjustment , the score achieved (over 85 points, our quality criterion) and the rating (special).

A well-done roast reveals the best in each bean in terms of flavors and aromas, generating a very high quality coffee, with substances that are good for health (caffeine, vitamins, minerals, amino acids, antioxidant polyphenols). A poorly done roast can ruin everything; spoiling good beans and resulting in lower quality coffee.

O gran finale. Os tipos de moedor, os diferentes métodos de extração (filtrado, espresso, prensa francesa, aeropress, moka...), a qualidade da água (filtrada ou mineral), a dosagem correta de cada ingrediente, as misturas criativas e o jeito que só você - consumidor - gosta de preparar e tomar o seu café (em casa, no trabalho, no restaurante, na cafeteria, na padaria...). Onde o ciclo termina e recomeça. Numa sequência de ações positivas que transformam pessoas, melhoram práticas e rendem bons frutos.

Every time you make a purchase on our website, you consume quality coffee and help small producers in the specialty coffee chain in Brazil. We invest annually in projects with a positive socio-environmental impact, leading producers to become aware of their own product.
