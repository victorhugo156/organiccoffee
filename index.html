About us

Organic Coffee is a coffee club with a distinguished selection of specialty coffees. We choose the best batches of the crop, roast it by hand and ship it to your home every month, always fresh. Choose whether you prefer to receive it in grains or already ground, taste the medium roast and the medium-light roast. Discover the taste of the best coffees of South America.

Awards

In 2021, for the 29th consecutive time, Organic was one of the winners of the Cup of Excellence, an international contest considered the Oscar of Special Coffee. His microlot of the Geisha variety delighted the judges and was rated at 87.47 points.

What is
coffee grinding?

Grinding is the process in which we subject the coffee bean to break it into particles, which can vary in size and thickness (granulometry), from extra fine (pulverized) to super coarse (as in crystal sugar beans).
